# Career Craft Youth

Career Craft Youth provides psychometric career counselling, stream selection guidance, admission strategy, study abroad planning, scholarship guidance, internship support, and OCI/NRI admission guidance for students and parents.

Canonical domain: https://careercraftyouth.in

## Who We Serve

- Students in grades 8 to 12
- Parents planning academic and career pathways
- Indian students exploring professional streams
- NRI, OCI, PIO, and Global Indian families seeking admission guidance
- Students exploring scholarships, internships, study abroad, and profile building

## Core Services

- Psychometric career counselling for students
- Stream discovery for grades 8 to 12
- Admission strategy for Indian and international colleges
- Study abroad guidance
- OCI, PIO, NRI, and CIWG admission guidance
- Scholarship discovery and application guidance
- Internship and research opportunity support
- Professional stream guidance for engineering, medical, law, management, design, and related fields
